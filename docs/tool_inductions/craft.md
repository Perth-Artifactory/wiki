## Ultrasonic Knife

| Item  | Requirement | Notes  |
| - | - | - |
| Eye protection (impact/splash) | M | Material dependant, knife pressure may cause flying shards |
| Ear protection                 | M | High pitched whine |

Allowed materials:

* Plastic
* Vinyl
* Fabric
* Foam
* Paper
* Cardboard
* Thin light wood (Balsa etc)

Specifically banned materials:

* Metal

Consumables:

* Blades - Purchased at bar (operator stores their own)

Pre:

1. Fit blade to handle (Blades are sharp even when not running)
1. Rotate battery 90d clockwise to engage (light should come on at the back)

Using:

1. Slide switch into position (momentary vs locked)
1. When cutting assume knife may cut faster than expected (don't put things you care about in the cut path)

After:

1. Disengage battery
1. Remove blade (will be hot if just used)
1. Put knife on charge after heavy use
