# Initial laser session

You've now finished the first part of your laser training, hooray! It's a lot of information to take in all at once so here's some resources to recap:

* [Laser checklist](https://wiki.artifactory.org.au/en/tools/lasers/operation)
* [Lightburn guide](https://wiki.artifactory.org.au/tools/lasers/lightburn)
* [Material advice](https://wiki.artifactory.org.au/tools/lasers/materials)

Your next steps are to:

* Review the material above
* Decide whether you're ready for your laser assessment. Your assessor will supervise you going through a job from start to finish and confirm that you're able to do so without putting yourself, others, or the machine in danger. They'll also check your knowledge of our fire and chlorine safety.
  * **If you're ready**: Book in for your assessment through #training-and-inductions! When you post in the thread for the day in question make sure you specify that you're looking for a laser assessment, not an intial training. You should also **mention who trained you** since we try to ensure you're trained and assessed by different people.
  * **If you're not sure yet**: You're welcome to book a second "first" session in using the training channel listed above. It's completely fine to want a second session to explore the machine in greater depth while there's still guardrails.
