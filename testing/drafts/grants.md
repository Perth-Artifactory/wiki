### Contact details

The Grants subcommittee can be contacted via email (grants@artifactory.org.au) or the [#grants channel on Slack](https://perthartifactory.slack.com/archives/CMPANM152).

### Role

To help the Artifactory identify grant opportunities, apply for grants, and manage grants after they are awarded.

### Terms of reference

1. Be the primary point of contact for grant providers (via the shared mailbox grants@artifactory.org.au).
2. Identify grant opportunities which are -
    - Relevant to the Artifactory's objects and purposes
    - Aligned to the Artifactory's capabilities
3. Maintain templates and other resources to support grant applications.
4. Prepare grant applications and submit grant applications.
    - Obtain committee approval before committing the Artifactory to a significant expenditure or a significant action.
5. Meet reporting and acquittal requirements for successful grants, working with the Treasurer as required.
6. Keep permanent records including -
    - A history of past, current, and prospective grant applications.
    - All information required to meet reporting requirements.
7. Report to the committee when requested.

### Record keeping

File all permanent records under the [Grants folder in the Artifactory's Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1a4JEqrM-sRBnQeOwWueyh8m2G4EKexj6).
